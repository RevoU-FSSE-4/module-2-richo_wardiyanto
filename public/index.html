Ecosystem Solana

Solana is a high-performance blockchain platform designed for decentralized applications (dApps) and crypto-currencies. It aims to provide scalability, speed, and security necessary for mass adoption of decentralized applications and crypto-currencies.

Pyth is a decentralized financial market data oracle built on the Solana blockchain. An oracle, in the context of blockchain technology, is a trusted source of information that feeds external data into smart contracts.

Raydium is a decentralized finance (DeFi) protocol built on the Solana blockchain. It serves as an automated market maker (AMM) and liquidity provider, enabling users to trade, swap, and provide liquidity for various crypto-assets.
